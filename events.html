Our Events



We primarily host NWSRS & USCF Swiss tournaments(FIDE soon!) every month or so, and we get a variety of players from beginners(100-300 USCF) to

high-ranked players(FIDE Masters, and other 1st/2nd category) players. Our tournaments are for everyone, and we give out prizes such as trophies and

cash prizes.

Most of our events are hosted at Microsoft Building 40 Cafe in Redmond, Washington.

15567 NE 31st St, Redmond, WA 98052

Many food options and free parking in the vicinity for players!
