Welcome to the Redmond Chess Champs Website!

A Washington State Based 501(c)(3) Non-profit Corporation in the making

Promoting Chess Events and activities in the Greater Seattle Area

Chess4All(Now RCC) was founded in 2023 by founder/president Sid Siddem & currently led by Sid and the RCC team dedicated to improving chess education in the Greater Seattle Area. Our goal is to provide various chess activities to interested players to learn and improve. Please do participate in our events if interested!

Check out the "Events" tab if you are interested in participating in our events!
